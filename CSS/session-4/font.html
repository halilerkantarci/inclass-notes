Top 22 Best Pubs and Nightclubs in Bangalore, 2022

Bangalore is India’s most famous city that comes alive when the sun goes down with several eclectic pubs. From dazzling trance beats trippy lights to yummilicious food, the Pubs in Bengaluru lets you unwind a hectic day attracting hoppers and partygoers equally. There are many exciting places to grab a drink across Bengaluru with servings of freshly brewed Alcohol, experiments of cocktails, and much more. So if you are looking for a place to party hard after a tiring day, check out these pubs in Bengaluru.

Top 20+ Night Clubs and Pubs In Bengaluru, Karnataka 2022:

1. Toit Brewpub:

Suppose you are looking to taste one of the best-known microbreweries in Indiranagar, Bengaluru. In that case, TOIT Pub is the first suggestion you would get. “Namma Beeru” is the latest addition to their experiments with beer with the already popular ones like rice draft, banana draft, and many more. Depending on the crowd, you can get a high chair, and the place has a cozy ambience. You can get different kinds of pasta, pizzas, old pub appetizers with a mix of South Indian accompaniments that complement the beer. TOIT is Bangalore’s popular pet-friendly pub in Bangalore with no entry fee and helpful staff.
